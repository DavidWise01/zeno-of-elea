# ZENO OF ELEA

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

ZENO OF ELEA (c. 490–430 BCE) — LOGIKĒ, the FRONT, pupil of [[parmenides]] and (per Aristotle) the INVENTOR OF DIALECTIC. To defend his teacher's changeless One, Zeno forged a devastating new weapon — the PARADOX, a proof by contradiction aimed at common sense: grant that space &amp; time are infinitely divisible and MOTION DISSOLVES INTO ABSURDITY. Swift Achilles can never catch a tortoise with a head start (each time he reaches where it was, it has crept further); an arrow in flight is, at each instant, at REST. These were the first arguments to take INFINITY seriously, and they were not truly answered for two thousand years — until the CALCULUS showed an unending sum of shrinking steps can have a FINITE total (½+¼+⅛+…=1).
